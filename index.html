Who we are

Your partner in print.

Chemo Graphic International is a specialist supplier of offset printing consumables, press room chemistry, blankets and related products for commercial, currency and security document printing. Our products are produced to the highest quality, taking into consideration security and currency printing standards as well as commercial and web printing requirements.

We provide our national and international customers with an extensive product and consultancy portfolio — from press room chemistry and printing blankets to vision inspection systems and security-printed labels. We believe a company should be a place of inspiration and creativity, combined with excellent quality and first-class service.

  • Commercial, web & newspaper printing

What we offer

Our Product Portfolio

A comprehensive range of consumables and equipment for every printing system — from intaglio and offset to web and newspaper.

Press Room Chemistry

Fountain solutions, washes, coatings and pressroom chemicals optimised for clean, consistent printing.

Printing Blankets

High-performance offset rubber blankets for commercial, security and web printing applications.

Security & Banknote Inks

Inks and varnishes for banknotes and security documents, meeting currency printing standards.

Security Papers

Paper for banknotes and security documents, plus calibrated under-packing paper and pressphan board.

Vision Inspection Systems

Automated inspection and quality-control systems for high-security and commercial production lines.

Spray Powder & Consumables

Anti set-off spray powders, tax stamps, labels and a full range of press room consumables.
